Desserts

A compact recipe compendium

A practical sweep through the desserts that come to mind first: baked, chilled, fried, frozen, spooned, sliced, and eaten straight from the tray while pretending it still counts as plating.

Cakes

Chocolate Fudge Cake

Ingredients: 200g flour, 200g sugar, 50g cocoa, 1 tsp baking powder, 2 eggs, 120ml oil, 180ml milk, 120ml hot coffee, ganache.

Method: Whisk dry ingredients, add eggs, oil, and milk, then stir in coffee. Bake at 180C for 30-35 minutes and cover with ganache.

Victoria Sponge

Ingredients: 200g butter, 200g sugar, 4 eggs, 200g self-raising flour, vanilla, jam, whipped cream.

Method: Cream butter and sugar, beat in eggs, fold in flour and vanilla. Bake two tins at 180C for 20 minutes, then sandwich with jam and cream.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients: 220g grated carrot, 180g flour, 150g brown sugar, 2 eggs, 120ml oil, cinnamon, walnuts, cream-cheese frosting.

Method: Mix wet and dry ingredients, fold in carrot and walnuts, bake at 175C for 35 minutes, cool, then frost generously.

Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients: 175g butter, 175g sugar, 3 eggs, 175g self-raising flour, 2 lemons, 85g icing sugar.

Method: Make a basic sponge with lemon zest, bake at 180C for 35 minutes, then pour over lemon juice mixed with icing sugar while warm.

Basque Cheesecake

Ingredients: 600g cream cheese, 200g sugar, 4 eggs, 300ml cream, 25g flour, vanilla, salt.

Method: Beat until smooth, pour into a lined tin, and bake at 220C for 30-35 minutes until deeply browned and still wobbly.

Banana Bread

Ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, 100g melted butter, 150g sugar, 1 egg, 190g flour, 1 tsp baking soda, salt.

Method: Mash bananas, stir in wet ingredients, fold in dry ingredients, and bake in a loaf tin at 175C for 50-60 minutes.

Pies

Apple Pie

Ingredients: shortcrust pastry, 6 apples, 90g sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, 20g flour, butter.

Method: Toss apples with sugar, cinnamon, lemon, and flour. Fill pastry, dot with butter, top, crimp, and bake at 190C for 45 minutes.

Key Lime Pie

Ingredients: biscuit crust, 4 egg yolks, 400g condensed milk, 120ml lime juice, lime zest, whipped cream.

Method: Whisk yolks, condensed milk, lime juice, and zest, pour into crust, bake at 170C for 15 minutes, chill, and top with cream.

Pecan Pie

Ingredients: pie crust, 200g pecans, 3 eggs, 180ml golden syrup, 120g brown sugar, 50g butter, vanilla.

Method: Whisk filling, stir in pecans, pour into crust, and bake at 180C for 45-50 minutes until just set.

Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients: baked pastry shell, lemon curd filling, 3 egg whites, 150g sugar, cream of tartar.

Method: Fill shell with warm lemon filling, whip whites with sugar to glossy peaks, spread on top, and bake at 180C until browned.

Puddings

Tiramisu

Ingredients: ladyfingers, espresso, 3 eggs, 90g sugar, 250g mascarpone, cocoa, optional marsala.

Method: Whip yolks with sugar, fold in mascarpone and whipped whites. Layer coffee-dipped ladyfingers with cream, chill, and dust with cocoa.

Creme Brulee

Ingredients: 500ml cream, 5 egg yolks, 70g sugar, vanilla, extra sugar for topping.

Method: Make custard, bake in a water bath at 150C until barely set, chill, then torch a thin sugar crust before serving.

Sticky Toffee Pudding

Ingredients: dates, hot water, baking soda, butter, brown sugar, eggs, flour, cream, treacle.

Method: Soften dates, blend into batter, bake at 180C, and serve soaked with hot toffee sauce made from cream, butter, and sugar.

Rice Pudding

Ingredients: 100g pudding rice, 700ml milk, 60g sugar, vanilla, nutmeg, butter.

Method: Combine in a dish, dot with butter, grate nutmeg over the top, and bake at 150C for about 2 hours until creamy.

Panna Cotta

Ingredients: 500ml cream, 70g sugar, vanilla, 2.5 tsp gelatine, berries.

Method: Warm cream with sugar and vanilla, dissolve gelatine, pour into moulds, chill until set, and serve with berries.

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients: 180g dark chocolate, 3 eggs, 30g sugar, 150ml cream, pinch of salt.

Method: Melt chocolate, fold in yolks, whipped cream, and sugared egg whites. Chill for at least 3 hours.

Cookies & Bars

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients: 170g butter, 150g brown sugar, 80g white sugar, 1 egg, 250g flour, baking soda, chocolate chips.

Method: Cream butter and sugars, add egg, fold in dry ingredients and chips, chill, then bake at 180C for 10-12 minutes.

Brownies

Ingredients: 180g butter, 200g chocolate, 250g sugar, 3 eggs, 90g flour, 30g cocoa, salt.

Method: Melt butter and chocolate, whisk in sugar and eggs, fold in dry ingredients, and bake at 170C for 25 minutes.

Blondies

Ingredients: 170g butter, 220g brown sugar, 2 eggs, 190g flour, vanilla, white chocolate, salt.

Method: Stir melted butter with sugar, add eggs and vanilla, fold in flour and chocolate, then bake at 175C for 22-26 minutes.

Millionaire's Shortbread

Ingredients: shortbread base, caramel from condensed milk, butter, sugar, golden syrup, and a dark chocolate top.

Method: Bake shortbread, cook caramel until thick, spread over base, chill, then cover with melted chocolate.

Pastries

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients: enriched dough, butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, cream cheese, icing sugar.

Method: Roll dough with cinnamon sugar, slice, prove, bake at 180C for 20-25 minutes, then glaze while warm.

Profiteroles

Ingredients: choux pastry, whipped cream, dark chocolate, cream, butter.

Method: Pipe and bake choux at 200C until crisp, fill with cream, and pour over warm chocolate sauce.

Baklava

Ingredients: filo pastry, melted butter, walnuts or pistachios, cinnamon, honey syrup.

Method: Layer buttered filo with nuts, cut diamonds, bake until golden, and soak with lemony honey syrup.

Apple Crumble

Ingredients: apples, sugar, cinnamon, lemon, 150g flour, 90g butter, 90g brown sugar, oats.

Method: Bake spiced apples under rubbed-in crumble topping at 180C for 35-40 minutes until bubbling and crisp.

Frozen & Quick

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: 500ml cream, 250ml milk, 5 yolks, 120g sugar, vanilla, salt.

Method: Cook custard until it coats a spoon, chill completely, churn, and freeze until scoopable.

Sorbet

Ingredients: 500g fruit puree, 150g sugar syrup, lemon juice, pinch of salt.

Method: Balance puree with syrup and lemon, chill, churn, then freeze. Mango, raspberry, and lemon all work well.

Affogato

Ingredients: vanilla ice cream, hot espresso, shaved chocolate or biscotti.

Method: Put ice cream in a cold glass, pour over espresso at the table, and finish with chocolate.

Eton Mess

Ingredients: strawberries, meringues, whipped cream, sugar, lemon juice.

Method: Macerate berries with sugar and lemon, fold through cream with crushed meringue, and serve immediately.
